
FINAL ANALYSIS REPORT
=====================

Project Title:
Advanced Retail Sales Analytics with Forecasting

1. Dataset Overview
- Custom synthetic dataset created for a retail business
- Records: 13,152
- Date range: 2023-01-01 to 2025-12-31
- Dimensions included: date, region, category, marketing spend, discount, units sold, revenue, cost, profit, customer satisfaction

2. Business KPIs
- Total Revenue: $797,059,455.76
- Total Profit: $266,785,307.32
- Total Units Sold: 2,787,167
- Average Customer Satisfaction: 4.20/5
- Overall Profit Margin: 33.47%

3. Key Insights
- Highest revenue generating category: Electronics
- Highest profit generating region: West
- Best month by revenue: December 2025
- Revenue shows a clear seasonal increase during Q4 and year-end months
- Marketing spend and units sold are strongly related to revenue growth
- Discount strategies improve demand but should be balanced with profit margin

4. Predictive Analysis
- Model Used: RandomForestRegressor
- MAE: 1,140,163.64
- RMSE: 1,241,904.97
- R² Score: 0.5648
- Average forecasted monthly revenue for next 6 months: $22,206,835.57

5. Recommendations
- Increase marketing investment during high-conversion months
- Focus growth strategies on Electronics category
- Replicate best-performing sales practices from West region
- Use forecast values for budget planning and inventory management
- Improve dashboard adoption for decision-making during monthly reviews

6. Final Deliverables Generated
- Analysis report
- Interactive dashboard HTML file
- Forecast CSV
- Presentation PPTX
